# UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Alignment 🌍

The **Rapid Crisis Response (RCR)** system is engineered to solve real-world emergency orchestration challenges in the hospitality sector, directly contributing to the following UN Sustainable Development Goals:

---

## 🎯 Primary Goals

### [Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being](https://sdgs.un.org/goals/goal3)
*Target 3.6: Halve the number of global deaths and injuries from road traffic accidents (and general emergencies).*
- **RCR Contribution:** By reducing response times by **70%** through automated AI triage, RCR ensures that medical emergencies in large-scale resorts are addressed within the "Golden Hour," significantly increasing survival rates.

### [Goal 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure](https://sdgs.un.org/goals/goal9)
*Target 9.1: Develop quality, reliable, sustainable and resilient infrastructure.*
- **RCR Contribution:** Our **Offline-First Edge AI** architecture ensures that the safety infrastructure remains operational even during network blackouts or natural disasters, creating a resilient safety net for high-occupancy buildings.

### [Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities](https://sdgs.un.org/goals/goal11)
*Target 11.5: Significantly reduce the number of deaths and the number of people affected by disasters.*
- **RCR Contribution:** RCR's **Z-Axis Indoor Routing** logic provides safe evacuation paths for guests in multi-story buildings, reducing panic-induced injuries during fires or structural failures.
